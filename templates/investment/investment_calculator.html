Investment Tips
SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)

SIP allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly, helping you benefit from rupee cost averaging and compound interest over time.

Lump Sum Investment

Lump sum investments involve investing a large amount at once, suitable when you have a significant amount available.

Expected Returns

Historical returns: Equity funds (10-15%), Debt funds (6-8%), Hybrid funds (8-12%. Past performance doesn't guarantee future results.

Risk Management

Diversify your investments across different asset classes and investment periods to manage risk effectively.
